Dog DNA research has revealed that the history of dog domestication began 11,000 years ago - back in the Paleolithic.

The dog has become man's best friend thanks to his loyalty, understanding, tolerance and ability to cooperate. Dogs are social animals that feel emotions and can recognize our emotions. Getting a dog is a responsible decision that requires a lot of time and effort. Here are some advantages and disadvantages of owning a dog:

Advantages:

1) Dogs can be fun and friendly, and they can become your best friends .

2) They can help you maintain an active lifestyle as they require regular walking .

3) Dogs can help reduce the risk of depression and loneliness .

4) They can protect you and your property from criminals.

Disadvantages:

1) Dogs need a lot of time and attention to be happy and healthy.

2) Owning a dog can be expensive as you have to provide them with enough food, veterinary care and other things.

3) They can be noisy and disturb the neighbors.

4) Dogs can be dangerous if not properly trained.

5)If you decide to get a dog, be prepared for the responsibility and provide them with enough attention and care.
