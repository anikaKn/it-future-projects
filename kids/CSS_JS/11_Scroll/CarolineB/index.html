❤️Potato - what makes the world such a great place❤️

Potatos are rich in vitamin C, which is an antioxidant. Potatoes were a life-saving food source in early times because the vitamin C prevented scurvy.

Nobody knows who invented the potato chip

While there are a handful of stories out there concerning the origin of the iconic snack, the reality is that nobody can really prove exactly where potato chips came from. The most common tale claims that potato chips were invented in 1853, in Saratoga, New York, completely by mistake.

Potatoes were the first vegetable grown in space

WNASA began a collaboration with the University of Wisconsin to study how potatoes grow in a zero-gravity environment. The experiment didn't result in full-grown potatoes but it did create a foundation for today's results. Today's International Space Station astronauts can eat micro potatoes, called Quantum Tubers.
